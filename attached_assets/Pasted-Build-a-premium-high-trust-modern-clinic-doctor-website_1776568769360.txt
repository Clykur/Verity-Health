Build a premium, high-trust, modern clinic/doctor website using Next.js (App Router), Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion.

Goal:
Create a clean, Apple-level, medical-grade website that builds instant trust and converts visitors into appointment bookings.

Brand Context:
- Location: Bangalore, India
- Audience: high-value patients, professionals, families
- Tone: calm, minimal, trustworthy, premium (not flashy like gyms)

Design System:
- Color palette: white, soft gray, light blue, subtle green accents
- Typography: Inter / SF Pro style (clean, readable, modern)
- Use lots of white space (Apple-like spacing)
- Rounded corners, soft shadows, glass effects
- Smooth, subtle animations (no aggressive motion)

Images:
- Use high-quality images from Pexels:
   - Doctors consulting patients
   - Clean clinic interiors
   - Medical equipment (modern)
   - Happy patients
- Avoid stocky/artificial images — choose realistic and premium
